Find – the – gem

In this competition, the teams need to make an automated locomotive bot that can navigate through a maze and collect the maximum number of gems they find on the route using computer vision in minimum time.

Stack-It-Up

In this competition, the teams need to make a remote-controlled (wired or wireless) locomotive bot that can pick up solid shapes (cube, cuboid, frustum, cone, cylinder) of different sizes from a place & stack them up one by one in a certain specified order in the given time frame.

Robowar

The Challenge is to design and build a robot competent to fight other robots in the Battle Arena. The competition is carried out in a tournament format in which the competitors bring their wired/wireless RC (Remote Controlled) Robots ready to battle and are pitted against their competitor’s robots in a one-one match where the aim is to cause maximum damage or to push the opponent’s robot off the arena to score maximum points. The 2 teams scoring the maximum points advances to finals of championship.

Codeloop

Codeloop is a knockout competition organised on the theme of traversal of a binary tree. Participants will take part in a knockout tournament where in every round two participants will be matched up against another participant. The winner will advance to the next round and the other participant will be eliminated from the competition. The last person standing will be declared as the winner of the competition

Fall-Stack
